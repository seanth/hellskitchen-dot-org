Taco Bout It

Podcasts by Gracies Dinnertime Theatre

At some point during a dinner at Gracies, someone said something out loud that probably should have stayed in their head. This has been the founding principle of Gracies Dinnertime Theatre since 1995. It seemed only natural to eventually find a format where that is entire point.

Taco Bout It is the umbrella under which GDT audio projects live. Whether that turns out to be one podcast or several, a single episode or an ongoing series, something scripted or something that simply got out of hand — it will be here.
